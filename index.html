WHO WE ARE

First Line Relief Initiative (FLRI) is a humanitarian organization dedicated to providing life-saving assistance, fostering resilience, and supporting sustainable development across Africa. We collaborate with communities, local governments, and global partners to deliver targeted interventions that uphold dignity, protect human rights, and ensure equitable access to essential services.
