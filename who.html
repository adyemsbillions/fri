WHO WE ARE

First Line Relief Initiative (FLRI) is a humanitarian organization dedicated to providing life-saving assistance, fostering resilience, and supporting sustainable development across Africa.

We collaborate with communities, local governments, and global partners to deliver targeted interventions that uphold dignity, protect human rights, and ensure equitable access to essential services.

Our Mission

Igniting hope and rebuilding lives. To provide life-saving assistance, promote sustainable development, and empower vulnerable populations through integrated humanitarian efforts in water and sanitation, shelter, protection, environmental management, education, food security, and healthcare.

Our Vision

To create an inclusive and thriving society where every individual has equal and equitable access to fundamental rights, dignity, and opportunities for a better life.

Our Core Values

Accountability We are responsible stewards of resources and transparent in our actions.
Transparency We communicate openly about our work, finances, and impact.
Integrity We act with honesty, ethics, and moral courage in everything we do.
Humanity We recognize the dignity and worth of every person affected by crisis or poverty.
Sustainability We design programs that build long-term resilience and self-reliance.
Faith We are guided by compassion, hope, and belief in positive change.
